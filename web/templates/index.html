Help & Shortcuts

Getting Started

Add Feed: Click "Add Feed" and enter a website URL or RSS feed URL
Import OPML: Click "Import OPML" to import feeds from other RSS readers like Feedly, Inoreader, or NewsBlur
Navigation: Use keyboard shortcuts or click to navigate between articles

Keyboard Shortcuts

j Next article
k Previous article
o or Enter Open article in new tab
m Toggle read/unread
s Toggle star
r Refresh feeds

Features

Personal Data: Your read/starred status and feed subscriptions are private to you
Auto-Read: Articles are automatically marked as read when you navigate away
Feed Discovery: Enter any website URL and we'll try to find its RSS feed
