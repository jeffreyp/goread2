Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 7, 2025

Information We Collect

GoRead2 collects minimal personal information necessary to provide our RSS feed reading service and process authentication.

Google Authentication

When you sign in with Google, we only access:

  • Your email address (for account identification)
  • Your name (for display purposes)
  • Your profile picture (for display purposes)

Data Storage

The only data we store is:

  • Your RSS feed subscriptions
  • Article read/unread status
  • Article starred status
  • Basic authentication information from Google
  • Subscription status and payment history (for Pro subscribers)

Payment Processing

For GoRead2 Pro subscriptions, we use Stripe to process payments securely. When you subscribe:

  • Data shared with Stripe: Your email address and name are shared with Stripe to create your customer account
  • Payment information: All payment card details are handled directly by Stripe and never stored on our servers
  • Stripe's role: Stripe acts as our payment processor and is subject to their own privacy policy
  • Data retention: Stripe retains payment and customer data according to their data retention policies and legal requirements

For more information about how Stripe handles your data, please review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Data Usage

We use your information solely to provide the RSS feed reading service and process subscription payments. Beyond our payment processor (Stripe), we do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to other third parties.

Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

Subscription data: If you have a paid subscription, some payment-related data may be retained by Stripe for legal and regulatory compliance even after account deletion. This includes transaction records required for tax purposes and fraud prevention.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your RSS feed subscriptions and data
  • Subscription management: Cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or Stripe's customer portal

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact us:
